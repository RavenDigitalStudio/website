Camp Shalom

It is with a heavy heart that we must announce that we are not holding Camp Shalom this summer. The ongoing uncertainty and slow rollout of vaccinations preclude us from committing to having the camp in Summer 2021. We are very sad that we will not see all of our friends this summer but we appreciate your continued support in the only Jewish camp in Tallahassee. We are 23 years strong and we will be back in 2022 even stronger.

Location: Temple Israel 2215 Mahan Drive, Tallahassee
Camp hours: 9:00am - 4:00pm daily (extended hours 7:30am-5:30pm)
